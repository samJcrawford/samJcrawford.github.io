The naturality of a locally covariant quantum field can be expressed as the equation

\[\mathfrak{A} \chi \Phi_\mathcal{M}(f) = \Phi_\mathcal{N}(\mathfrak{D} \chi f)\]

or equivalently in terms of the diagram

\[\require{AMScd} \begin{CD} \mathfrak{D}(\mathcal{M}) @>\Phi_\mathcal{M}>> \mathfrak{A}(\mathcal{M})\\ @VV\mathfrak{D}\chi V @VV\mathfrak{A}\chi V\\ \mathfrak{D}(\mathcal{N}) @>\Phi_\mathcal{N}>> \mathfrak{A}(\mathcal{N}) \end{CD}\]

which itself may be expressed using the short hand \(\Phi: \mathfrak{D} \Rightarrow \mathfrak{A}\)
